  • ALS/FTD-Linked Mutation in FUS Suppresses Intra-axonal Protein Synthesis and Drives Disease Without Nuclear Loss-of-Function of FUS. Lopez-Erauskin et al. Neuron (2018), https://doi.org/10.1016/j.neuron.2018.09.044 🧠
    • This paper describes that in amyotrophic lateral sclerosis (ALS) pathogenesis, toxicity, not loss of function, of ALS/FTD-linked mutant FUS drives disease and that intra-axonal protein synthesis is inhibited by ALS/FTD-causing mutants in FUS.

Da Cruz, S., Parone, P.A., Lopes, V.S., Lillo, C., McAlonis-Downes, M., Lee, S.K., Vetto, A.P., Petrosyan, S., Marsala, M., Murphy, A.N., Williams, D.S., Spiegelman, B.M., and Cleveland, D.W. Cell
